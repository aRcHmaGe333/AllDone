## What this should buy

Within 12-18 months, this capital should aim to produce:

- one real pilot environment
- one functioning container loop
- one validated wash and return process
- one operator-facing software flow
- first live operating metrics

## What it should not fund first

- broad consumer marketing
- large team growth before loop proof
- wide geography expansion
- polish that hides weak operations
