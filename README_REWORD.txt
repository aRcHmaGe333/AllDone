AllDone  
A closed-loop system for home provisioning designed with reusable containers, return logistics, industrial washing, and recirculation.

AllDone is an idea and a prototype project aimed at replacing the need for repetitive household shopping with a managed supply loop. Goods come in reusable containers, quantities adjust over time, and these containers are picked up, cleaned, refilled, and put back into circulation.

The standard container for consumers is reusable glass.

What AllDone Is  
AllDone goes beyond being just a delivery app.  

It is a fuller operating model:  

production -> packaging -> circulation -> home supply -> return -> reuse  

The project focuses on recurring household needs, including:

- Groceries and prepared meals  
- Personal hygiene items  
- Household essentials  
- Other goods that fit a return-and-reuse model  

Why It Matters  
The current retail system wastes:

- Consumer time  
- Packaging materials  
- Transportation efforts  
- Food due to mismatches and overbuying  
- Labor through disconnected shopping habits and last-mile delivery  

AllDone aims to eliminate the burden of these chores rather than just slightly improving the existing disposable system.

Prototype Code  
The current codebase includes an early backend prototype for:

- Household consumption tracking  
- Adjusting quantities  
- Generating simple schedules  
- Modeling reusable container circulation  
- Workflow management for household operations, subscriptions, routes, cleaning, and billing  
- Local file-based runtime data storage
