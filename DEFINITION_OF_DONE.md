# AllDone - Definition of Done

- The closed-loop supply flow is pilot-ready and documented end to end.
- The reusable container standard is fixed and reflected in the operating docs.
- Ordering, wash/return, and replenishment paths have been validated in a realistic pilot scenario.
- Public-facing material is aligned with the exposure and privacy boundaries.
- Obvious prototype residue, scratch logs, and duplicate notes are cleaned up or quarantined.
