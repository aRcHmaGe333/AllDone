# Gasket Service Policy

Status: approved for pilot planning

Purpose
-------
This document defines the first gasket service policy for the AllDone pilot.

It closes `P1.4`.

## Role of the gasket

The silicone gasket is the primary planned wear component in the closure path.

The system should replace gaskets before it replaces otherwise sound glass bodies and lids.

## Policy

- inspect gaskets at each post-wash inspection
- replace immediately if torn, permanently deformed, odor-retaining, or compression-failed
- keep spare gaskets in pilot inventory by SKU

## Working lifecycle assumption

Initial planning assumption:

- target service range: approximately `100-200` cycles

This remains a planning assumption until live wash chemistry and handling data exist.

## Inspection triggers

- visible tear or cut
- poor reseal performance
- persistent odor retention
- flattening or loss of elastic rebound

## Stocking assumption

- keep replacement stock for at least `10-15%` of active pilot gasket count at any time

## Why this policy matters

- keeps the passive glass body in circulation longer
- simplifies sanitation confidence
- gives the pilot a controlled wear component instead of uncontrolled closure degradation
